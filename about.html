ABOUT ME

I‘m a hybrid UX Designer based in Miami by way of Singapore.

I work to design, develop, and integrate user-driven digital products within the hospitality space.

I am passionate about finding innovative ways to leverage technology to provide sophisticated and delightful experiences.

Besides wearing my UX hat, you will often find me organizing (anything and everything), creating simple dishes, discovering the best restaurants around the world, working with my hands, and enjoying the sunshine.

Few things bring me such joy as my niece, my chili plants, and my one and only.

With 10 years of experience across multiple design disciplines – UX, UI, Visual, Marketing, Branding, Product – I have the ability to look at design problems from many angles.

Every element of my background is part of a bigger picture of understanding the user and using that understanding to inform design decisions. I take that big picture approach to solving design problems, incorporating strategy, research, analysis, ideation, prototyping, testing and iterating to create solutions that push to balance user experience and business needs through the entire creative process.

My sweet spot is the user - experiences and interfaces. My strengths are best used working with small to medium collaborative and international teams looking to design digital products to enhance their users’ experiences.
